What is a Discount Calculator?

A Discount Calculator helps you quickly determine the final price after applying one or more discounts. Whether it's a percentage off, a fixed amount, stacked discounts, or reverse‑engineering the original price, this tool gives you instant results including tax and per‑item breakdowns. Essential for shoppers, retailers, and business owners.

How to Use the Discount Calculator (Step-by-Step Guide)

Step 1 Choose a mode: % off, fixed, reverse, stacked, profit, or bulk.
Step 2 Enter the original price / cost.
Step 3 Fill in discount details (percentage, amount, second discount, etc.).
Step 4 Add tax % and quantity if needed.
Step 5 View final price, savings, and detailed breakdown.

How Discount is Calculated (Formulas)

% off: Discount = Price × (rate/100); Final = Price – Discount
Fixed off: Final = Price – fixed amount
Stacked: First discount applied, then second discount on new price
Reverse: Original = Sale price / (1 – discount%)
Profit mode: Profit = Selling – Cost; Margin = Profit / Selling

Examples of Discount Calculations
