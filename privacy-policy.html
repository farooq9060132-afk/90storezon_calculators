Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2024

Introduction

At 90StoreZon, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the FINAL BEST CALCULATORS on our website.

Information We Collect

Our calculators are designed to work entirely in your browser without collecting or storing your personal calculation data. We may collect:

  • Basic analytics data (page views, browser type, device type) through standard web analytics
  • Information you voluntarily provide through our contact form
  • Cookies for website functionality and analytics

How We Use Your Information

Any information we collect is used solely for:

  • Improving the FINAL BEST CALCULATORS and website functionality
  • Responding to your inquiries and support requests
  • Understanding how visitors use our website
  • Maintaining website security and preventing abuse

Calculator Data Privacy

All calculations performed using the FINAL BEST CALCULATORS happen entirely in your browser. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your calculation inputs or results to our servers. Your calculation data remains completely private and secure on your device.

Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience and analyze website traffic. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. Disabling cookies may affect some website functionality.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for analytics and advertising. These services may collect information about your visits to our website. We recommend reviewing their privacy policies for more information.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of information. However, no internet transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy

Our website and the FINAL BEST CALCULATORS are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your information, please contact us through our contact page.
